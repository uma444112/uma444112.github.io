COLLABORATE . CONNECT . CONSULT

_________ We are a student-led consulting organization on campus dedicated to facilitating the transition of engineering graduates into the world of management consulting. Through collaborative teamwork, strategic thinking, and practical experience, we prepare our members to excel in consulting careers.

Join a vibrant community where brainstorming over coffee, peer-led practice cases, and late-night strategy sessions are part of the journey.
Whether you’re aiming for a top consulting firm or launching your own venture, our club gives you the toolkit and network to turn ambition into achievement.

We are a student-led consulting organization on campus dedicated to facilitating the transition of engineering graduates into the world of management consulting.

Through collaborative teamwork, strategic thinking, and practical experience, we prepare our members to excel in consulting careers.

Join a vibrant community where brainstorming over coffee, peer-led practice cases, and late-night strategy sessions are part of the journey.

DOMAINS:
1.

Consulting Projects

Jump into real projects and work with a team to solve actual business challenges—get hands-on experience from day one!

2.

Professional Development

Boost your skills with fun workshops on problem-solving, analytics, and digital tools—everything you need to shine in any field.

3.

Public Relationships

Help share our story! Create cool posts, manage our social media, and connect with other clubs and students across campus.

4.

Human Resources

Be part of making our club a friendly, supportive space. Organize events, welcome new members, and keep everyone connected.

5.

Corporate Outreach

Meet industry professionals, bring exciting opportunities to the club, and help us build valuable partnerships beyond campus.

6.

The Future Is Yours !

Challenge yourself, discover new strengths, and shape your future with a community that supports your ambitions.
